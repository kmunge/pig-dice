Rule of the game!

gaming guidelines!

The required number of players requiredis two

  • For each player's round,one can roll the dice as many times as they want
  • The goal is to accumulate as many points as possible,adding up the numbers rolled on the die.
  • If a player rolls a 1 on the die,the player's turn is over and any points they have accumulated get forfeited.
  • A player can also choose to hold(stop rolling the dice) to avoid the chance of rolling 1 and forfeiting there points
  • If the player holds the points accumulated are added to their score,and next player takes turn
  • The first player to reach a total of 100 points wins the game and it Ends!!
